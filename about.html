About Avionero

Travel planning that is better for you, and for the world.

Our system uses a custom made AI search engine (energy efficient and not LLM based) that looks through the world’s largest selection of flights to find the best deals matching with your particular preferences. Whether your dates are fixed or wide open, whether you’re flying to Paris or just chasing sunshine, our search technology makes it easy to find the best options available.

Since travel planning isn't only about flights, we also offer hotels and experiences matched with each suggested flight option. No need to re-enter location and dates somewhere else. All for your convenience.\r\n\r\nBut we don’t stop there.

We then donate 10% of our revenue to a charity you choose after booking; so every trip you take helps someone else move forward too.

It’s just a better way to search for flights. For you, and for the world.
